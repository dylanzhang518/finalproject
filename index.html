Why Recycle?

The United States produces more electronic waste annually than any other country, a total of 9.4 million tons.

Recycling electronics prevents valuable, limited materials fom going into the waste stream and landfills. Recycling reduces the overall energy use and pollution that would be created while manufactring new products. Recycling allows everyday consumers, retailers, and municipalities to reduce their everyday environmental footprint.

What Can I Recycle?

Local recycling facilities differ in particular items which can be recycled, however most locations recycle lamps, lightbulbs, miscellaneous electronics.
